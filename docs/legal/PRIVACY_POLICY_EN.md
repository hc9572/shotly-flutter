# Shotly Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 27, 2026

Shotly is a screenshot organization app designed to help users find and organize screenshots stored on their device. Shotly does not upload original screenshots, thumbnails, file names, file paths, notes, folder names, or search queries to Shotly servers. Shotly may, however, collect limited app usage logs and error logs to improve stability, troubleshoot issues, and understand product usage.

This Privacy Policy is written for international users and is intended to address common privacy requirements such as transparency, data minimization, retention, user rights, and international data transfers. Depending on your location, you may have additional rights under laws such as the GDPR, UK GDPR, CCPA/CPRA, or other applicable privacy laws.

## 1. Information We Process

### 1.1 Information processed locally on your device
The following information is processed locally on your device to provide Shotly’s core features and is not uploaded to Shotly servers:

- Original screenshots and image files
- Thumbnail and preview cache files used to display images
- Image file names, file paths, creation dates, added dates, modified dates, and similar file metadata
- Visual feature data generated locally for similar image analysis
- User-configured app group names, folder names, notes, folder colors, hidden/restored status, pinned status, and sort mode
- Search queries entered in the app

### 1.2 Logs that may be collected by servers or analytics tools
Shotly may collect limited logs for app stability, troubleshooting, analytics, and product improvement, including:

- Anonymous installation ID or randomly generated app instance ID
- App version, build number, operating system version, device model, language, and country/region settings
- Product events such as app open, photo permission granted or denied, similar screen analysis started/completed/failed, backup exported/imported, original deletion requested/succeeded/failed
- Error codes, crash logs, performance logs, and timestamps
- Google Play purchase status, purchase country, and related purchase verification information when needed

Shotly is designed not to include original screenshots, thumbnails, file names, file paths, notes, folder names, search queries, or image content in server logs.

## 2. How We Use Information

Shotly may use information for the following purposes:

- Provide screenshot organization features
- Display, sort, search, group, hide, and organize screenshots locally on your device
- Provide similar screen analysis and cleanup suggestions
- Provide user-initiated backup and restore functionality
- Process user-requested sharing or original image deletion
- Verify paid app purchase status through Google Play when needed
- Improve app stability, diagnose bugs, analyze crashes, and improve product usability
- Prevent misuse and maintain service security
- Respond to support requests

## 3. Legal Bases for Processing

Where applicable, Shotly relies on the following legal bases:

- Performance of a contract: to provide the paid app and its core features
- Legitimate interests: to maintain app security, analyze errors, improve reliability, and understand aggregated product usage
- Consent: where required for analytics, device permissions, or similar technologies
- Legal obligation: where required for payment, tax, accounting, compliance, or lawful requests

## 4. Permissions

Shotly may request access to photos or media to provide screenshot organization features.

- Android 13 and later: `READ_MEDIA_IMAGES`
- Android 14 and later: limited access to photos selected by the user may apply
- Android 12 and earlier: `READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE`
- Purpose: displaying screenshot lists, generating thumbnails, analyzing similar images, organizing images in the app, and processing user-requested sharing or deletion
- If permission is denied: some or all screenshot organization features may not work

You can change or revoke permissions at any time through your device settings.

## 5. Paid App Purchases

Shotly may be sold as a paid app through Google Play. Google Play processes payments, refunds, receipts, taxes, and purchase history according to Google’s own terms and privacy policy. Shotly does not collect or store your full payment card details.

Shotly currently does not offer in-app purchases, subscriptions, or ads.

## 6. Backup and Restore

Shotly may provide user-initiated backup and restore functionality.

- Backup may include Shotly organization data such as app group names, folder names, notes, hidden status, pinned status, and sort mode.
- Backup does not include original screenshot image files.
- Backup files are exported by the user to a location or external app chosen by the user.

If you save or share a backup file using Google Drive, email, messaging apps, file apps, or another external service, that external service’s terms and privacy policy apply.

## 7. Sharing and Original Image Deletion

Shotly performs sharing or original image deletion only when explicitly requested by the user.

- Original deletion: if you choose to delete images and complete the operating system’s deletion confirmation process, the selected images may be deleted from your device gallery or file storage.
- In-app hiding or reclassification: hiding or moving images within Shotly changes their organization state inside the app and does not physically move or delete original files.
- Sharing: if you choose to share images, your device’s system share sheet may send the selected images to the external app or service you choose.

## 8. Service Providers and International Transfers

Shotly may use service providers for app distribution, purchase processing, analytics, crash reporting, log storage, hosting, or infrastructure. These providers may process information in countries other than your country of residence.

Potential providers may include:

- Google Play for paid app distribution, purchase processing, and refund handling
- Google Analytics for Firebase, Firebase Crashlytics, or similar analytics/error reporting tools for app usage logs, crash logs, and error diagnostics
- Cloud or server infrastructure providers for limited log storage and service operations

Transferred information is limited to what is necessary for app operation, analytics, crash reporting, and purchase verification. Shotly does not transfer original screenshots, thumbnails, file names, file paths, notes, folder names, search queries, or image content to these providers for analytics/logging purposes.

Where required by applicable law, Shotly will use appropriate safeguards for international transfers and will provide additional details about actual providers, countries, retention periods, and safeguards when finalized for production.

## 9. Retention

Shotly keeps information only as long as necessary for the purposes described in this Policy.

- Local organization data: stored on your device until you delete it or uninstall the app
- Backup files: stored wherever you choose to save them until you delete them
- App usage logs and error logs: retained for up to 180 days, then deleted or anonymized
- Purchase-related records: retained according to Google Play policies and applicable legal requirements
- Support communications: may be retained for up to 3 years after resolution

## 10. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, restrict, object to, or port your personal data. You may also have the right to withdraw consent where processing is based on consent.

You can:

- Grant, limit, or revoke photo/media permissions in your device settings
- Edit or delete local organization data within the app
- Delete the app to remove local app data, subject to your device settings
- Contact us about server logs, privacy rights, or privacy questions

Because Shotly currently does not provide user accounts, most local data controls are available directly on your device.

## 11. Children’s Privacy

Shotly is not intended to knowingly collect personal data from children under the age required by applicable law. If you believe a child has provided personal data through Shotly, please contact us.

## 12. Security

Shotly uses reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure. Users are responsible for safeguarding exported backup files and controlling where they are shared or stored.

## 13. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated to reflect changes to the app, operational practices, service providers, platform policies, or applicable laws. Material changes will be communicated through the app, app distribution page, or policy page when appropriate.
